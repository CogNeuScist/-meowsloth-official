# M.E.O.W.S.L.O.T.H ($MEOWS)

"In the flood of FOMO, stay conscious."

M.E.O.W.S.L.O.T.H. ($MEOWS) is not just a memecoin; it is a behavioral art experiment—a chimera of Ragdoll-cat agility and Sloth-zen. It is the world's first dynamic meme that "breathes" with the market's pulse.

## Core Philosophy
We navigate the digital landscape by balancing two extremes:
* **The Ragdoll:** Representing the agility to seize opportunities in a volatile market.
* **The Sloth:** Representing the meditative state of minimalism and avoiding the trap of over-competition.

M.E.O.W.S.L.O.T.H. utilizes a real-time evolution mechanism linked to the ETH 24h volatility. Our visual identity shifts based on the market's "metabolism":
